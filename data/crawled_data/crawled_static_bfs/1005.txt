tours guided tour with urban academy – august wilson: the writer’s landscape mar 15, 2025 - mar 15, 2025																   | 1:00 pm - 2:00 pm august wilson african american cultural center 980 liberty ave pittsburgh, pa 15222 event website experience the enduring legacy of august wilson with special guided tours lead by urban academy k-5 students step into the world of august wilson through the eyes of the next generation of writers. we are thrilled to partner with urban academy’s k-5 students to offer unique, guided tours of our immersive exhibition, august wilson: the writer’s landscape. experience the life and legacy of the legendary playwright, while hearing firsthand stories and insights from these young, creative minds. discover the power of storytelling and how wilson’s work continues to inspire and shape the future of theater. the exhibition has timed entry to ensure each guest has adequate time to experience the full impact of the exhibit. admission is free, but advanced registration is required. urban academy students will be leading tours on the following dates: march 15th from 1-2 pm april 5th from 1-2 pm june 7th from 1-2 pm downtown events view
