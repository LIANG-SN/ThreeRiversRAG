holidays kegs n' eggs at city works pittsburgh mar 15, 2025 - mar 15, 2025																   | 8:00 am - 11:00 am city works pittsburgh 2 ppg place pittsburgh, pa 15222 event website join us bright and early on march 15 for the best st. patrick's day shenanigans in pittsburgh. that’s right, kegs n' eggs is back and better than ever. it’s the ultimate pre-parade party that'll have you sham-rocking in the wee morning hours. tickets are just $25, but space is limited, so don't wait to secure your spot. whether you're a pittsburgh local or just visiting for the festivities, kegs n'
