culture, entertainment sound in motion mar 14, 2025 - mar 16, 2025 new hazlett theater 6 allegheny square east pittsburgh, pa 15212 event website texture contemporary ballet’s 14th season culminates in sound in motion , a captivating exploration of the interplay between movement and music. prepare for a live-music collaboration that ignites the stage: artistic director alan obuzor joins forces with pittsburgh-based musician, d.o.b., fusing edgy r&b music with the raw power and artistry of texture’s dancers. resident dancer madeline kendall schreiber invites you on a journey through the world’s diverse ecosystems in her new work, using a rich tapestry of instrumental music and the evocative lighting of bob steineck to create an immersive and transformative experience . rounding out the performance is a breathtaking piece set to j.s. bach’s concerto no. 1 in d minor, showcasing the dancers’ impeccable technique and profound connection to this timeless masterpiece. downtown events
