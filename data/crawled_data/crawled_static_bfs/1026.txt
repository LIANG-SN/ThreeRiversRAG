culture, entertainment, music jason kush mar 18, 2025 - mar 18, 2025																   | 5:00 pm - 7:00 pm lounge at the greer cabaret 655 penn ave pittsburgh, pa 15222 event website dr. jason kush is an acclaimed saxophonist, educator, and scholar. kush has performed as a leader, soloist, and ensemble member in various musical settings in the united states, europe, and central and south america. a genre-crossing performer, kush is a member of the pittsburgh jazz orchestra and has performed thirteen seasons with the pittsburgh symphony orchestra, including a european tour. as a freelance artist, kush has appeared with the woody herman orchestra, smithsonian jazz masterworks orchestra, new world symphony, russian national orchestra, maceo parker, arturo sandoval, eddie daniels, paquito d’rivera, benny golson, sean jones, christian mcbride, jeff “tain” watts, freddie cole, andrea bocelli, hugh jackman, and many others. kush’s saxophone talents can be heard on albums by the south florida jazz orchestra, the south nine ensemble, the balcony big band, the mike tomaro big band, the nathan douds ensemble, madd for tadd, and the stephen philip harvey jazz orchestra. kush’s most recent recording, finally friday, was released on mcg jazz in february 2023. kush’s other solo discography includes intersecting lines (new focus records/naxos) and sonate (armazi productions). kush has published works as an author and academic in various periodicals and journals. he currently holds music professorships at slippery rock university and carnegie mellon university. kush is endorsed by the conn-selmer and selmer paris companies. presented in partnership with mcg jazz. downtown events view all arts + culture community convention entertainment family festival film & movies holidays
