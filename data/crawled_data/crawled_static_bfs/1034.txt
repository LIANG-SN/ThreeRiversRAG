workshops & classes backstage/onstage with joe grushecky mar 20, 2025 - mar 20, 2025																   | 5:00 pm - 7:00 pm pittsburgh playhouse 350 forbes ave pittsburgh, pa 15222 event website held onstage of the pnc theatre join us for an inspiring conversation with joe grushecky , acclaimed pittsburgh musician and educator, as he shares his journey of keeping his passion for music alive. hosted by ed traversari , professor of sport, art, and entertainment management, this discussion will dive into grushecky’s latest musical project, featuring four songs co-written with his longtime friend and legendary producer, bruce springsteen . don’t miss this exclusive opportunity to hear about the creative process behind the music and the stories that shaped it! about backstage/onstage this new series is co-produced and hosted by media innovator andy conte, and entertainment expert ed traversari. backstage/onstage invites playhouse patrons behind the curtain into intimate and unique conversations with pittsburgh’s thought leaders across various industries. ticket price range $25 downtown events view all arts + culture community convention entertainment family festival film &
