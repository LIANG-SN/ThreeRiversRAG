culture, entertainment prismatic impressions mar 27, 2025 - mar 27, 2025																   | 7:00 pm - 10:00 pm new hazlett theater 6 allegheny square east pittsburgh, pa 15212 event website ballet legato is excited to be taking our repertoire on the road for our first out of state venture performing  for one night only on thursday, march 27th, 2025 at 7pm. ballet legato presents “prismatic impressions”, a colorful array of repertoire bringing the audience an eclectic prism of dance! repertoire will feature choreography by executive artistic director, jennifer muselin to include, “infoxication”, a look at understanding the complexity of mind information overload and the fun and energetic jazz piece, “hit play!” this showcase will also include choreographic works by ballet legato dancers in an array of stylistic impressions. we would love to get to know our pittsburgh dance enthusiasts so we hope to see you at the show! about ballet legato established in 2019, cleveland-based dance company, ballet legato hopes to offer a fresh new view of dance to our community through the direction of our executive artistic director.  dance brings people together and bridges generations. we want our performances and appearances to contribute to this togetherness – we see them as a way to give back to society and an expression of the individuality we each bring to our audience. ballet legato is proud to introduce artistic and innovative repertoire from classical to contemporary works while connecting with our community and familiarizing them with the depth and relevance of dance as an art form; presenting accessible performances of the highest quality while enriching the lives of the artists and audiences with a range of significant, ever-evolving dance.
