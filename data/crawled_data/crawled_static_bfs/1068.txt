get ready for another unforgettable night at city paper’s best theater in pgh of 2024, arcade comedy theater —where comedy titans, select start (city paper’s 2 time best comedy act) and latchkey kids (pittsburgh magazine’s 3 time best improv team), will join forces with chi chi de vivre (city paper’s best drag performer 2024) to celebrate all of their 2024 victories! these two arcade-born award-winning improv teams will deliver an electrifying blend of fast-paced short-form improv and 90s nostalgia-inspired comedy. chi chi de vivre will give an iconic drag performance. join us for an
