culture, entertainment storm large: inside voice mar 29, 2025 - mar 29, 2025																   | 7:00 pm - 10:00 pm greer cabaret theater 655 penn ave pittsburgh, pa 15222 event website international icon and multi-hyphenate musician, actor, playwright and performer, storm large has affixed herself as the queen of twisted beauty & subversive delights to a cult-like following of millions across the page, stage & screen. following a stratospheric rise from the portland club scene to the edinburgh fringe festival and sold-out runs at the kennedy center, large’s endeavors both as a soloist and with her bands – the balls and pink martini, then le bonheur – have installed her as a quintessential cabaret powerhouse through renditions of the american songbook, a one-women musical memoir in crazy enough, features on cbs’ rock star: supernova & america’s got talent, homages to the work of randy newman, and beyond. she’s a tour de force that’s shared the stage with the likes of joel grey, liza minnelli and michael feinstein. now, she brings her stellar talents to the state-of-the-art greer cabaret theater for one night only! downtown events view all arts + culture community convention
