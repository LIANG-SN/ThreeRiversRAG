the cmu alumni association the carnegie mellon university alumni association, in conjunction with the alumni association board , develops meaningful ways to help you connect with the cmu alumni community and current students as well as the university. we're here to listen, share information and resources, and offer a wide array of meaningful volunteer roles to you and all tartans around the world. through regional, identity and affinity networks; reunion committees; and college and school alumni activities, your relationship with carnegie mellon university can continue to deepen well
