recognition ›              parents leadership circle parents leadership circle (plc) the plc is a community of engaged parents who provide direct financial support of university programs for the benefit of carnegie mellon students. membership in the plc is conferred on the parents of current students who have made gifts totaling $25,000 or more, which may be fulfilled over five years. gifts made by members of the plc are designated to initiatives and programs that are meaningful to parents and families and their students. contact us adison ammons assistant director, donor relations adisona@andrew.cmu.edu 412-268-2462 "the parents leadership circle offers a tremendous opportunity to develop a deeper connection with cmu leadership, faculty and parents. we are enthusiastic about supporting cmu's mission to make a meaningful impact on the lives of students, our community and our world. go tartans!" annette motoyama and kelvin chow, parents of tpr 2025 student parents
