community of students, faculty and staff. thank you to our acs members for joining us as we advance cmu’s vision through philanthropy and engagement. moving toward the future since its founding, fellowships, candidates know that cmu is confident in their potential and invested in their success. new and increased resources, like the new carnegie mellon university cloud lab which harnesses the possibilities of automated science and accelerates the potential impact of the research being done at cmu. undergraduate awards that recognize and reward students who have overcome adversity in their
