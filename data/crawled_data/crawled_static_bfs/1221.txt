           working in university advancement working in university advancement university advancement at carnegie mellon is committed to people – both our employees and the constituents we serve. we are looking for talented individuals with a passion for their work and a dedication to the mission of cmu. members of the university advancement staff enjoy: a comprehensive benefits package competitive with other local and national employers executive and management coaching, and numerous professional development opportunities cross-practice collaboration initiatives a comprehensive onboarding program an opportunity to work in what’s consistently ranked one of america's most livable cities a cross-discipline staff committee that works closely with leadership to maintain a positive and productive work environment as one of pittsburgh’s premier employers, carnegie mellon values its employees and provides opportunities for them to maintain health and wellness, grow and
