community. get to know us by watching the video above, produced by our friends at marketspace communications. by supporting pittsburgh opera , you play a critical role in our region’s cultural vitality and growth: you enrich our city , making pittsburgh a beacon for world-class opera ranging from traditional favorites to provocative new music , and ensuring the highest level of artistic excellence, even while ticket sales cover less than half the real cost of opera. you foster rising talent , building opera stars of the future through the resident artist program , ranked in the top-five training programs nationally. you inspire the next generation , bringing educational workshops and student matinees to 20,000 young people, many of whom are experiencing opera for the very first time. you create opportunities for everyone , carrying opera into the community with free and approachable performances in diverse neighborhoods throughout our region. you build a community , meeting and mingling with fellow opera lovers at our special events to discover new friends and colleagues. you join a legacy , uniting with people who have led pittsburgh opera from its roots as a female-led organization to its prominence today as the seventh oldest company in the united states. pittsburgh opera is rated platinum , their highest rating, by industry-leading guidestar , an information service specializing in reporting on u.s. nonprofit companies. pittsburgh opera has been evaluated by charity navigator , the world’s largest and most trusted nonprofit evaluator, and earned a four-star rating. this is the highest possible rating on charity navigator. charity navigator’s third-party accreditation validates our organization’s operational excellence. contributions to pittsburgh opera are tax-deductible. the official registration and financial information of pittsburgh opera may be obtained from the pennsylvania department of state by calling toll-free, within
