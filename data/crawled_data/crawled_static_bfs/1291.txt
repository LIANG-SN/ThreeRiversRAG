form join friends of pittsburgh opera friends of pittsburgh opera (fpo) is both a friend-raising and fund-raising body in support of pittsburgh opera. membership in friends of pittsburgh opera is $75 per household of 2 or more, or $50 per individual. your membership fee supports pittsburgh opera financially, and your participation helps promote interest in, and awareness of, pittsburgh opera in the community. fpo membership benefits include: invitations to community events throughout the season invitations to fabulous social events like the holiday party and the garden party opportunities to
