center the andy warhol museum media resources resources carnegie museums of pittsburgh is the largest and most far-reaching cultural organization in pittsburgh. established in 1895 by andrew carnegie, carnegie museums is a collection of four distinctive museums: carnegie museum of art, carnegie museum of natural history, carnegie science center, and the andy warhol museum. we reach more than 1.4 million people a year through exhibitions, educational programs, outreach activities, and special events. request a 2024 media pass download our logo learn more about what makes us unique: our history
