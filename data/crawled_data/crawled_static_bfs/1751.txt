a husband and wife team, operating out of their home as a cottage kitchen. they will go over making a dough ball, feeding a starter, stretch and folds, proofing timelines, inclusions, shaping methods, banneton use and care, as well as baking methods. class participants will go home with a jar of starter, a silicone banneton, a lame, recipes, a link to an amazon list with all of your sourdough needs, and a dough ball! don't miss out on this exciting night of games, drinks, and fun prizes! and don't forget your bottle of kat de lac artist wine to take home. last chance the stories we keep: conserving objects from ancient egypt ⚱️🛕 📜 through mar 9 | carnegie museum of natural history the stories we keep features more than 80 ancient items—including the 4,000-year-old dahshur boat, one of only four in the world. engage with these objects like never before, have conversations with museum conservators, observe the care and restoration of objects in real time, and attempt the work yourself by reassembling replicas of ancient artifacts. ongoing… kara walker: harper's pictorial history of the civil war (annotated) 🖼️ mar 1-may 25 | frick pittsburgh comprised of 15 large scale prints, kara walker: harper’s pictorial history of the civil war (annotated) revisits the historical representations found in the two-volume anthology published in 1866, "harper’s pictorial history of the civil war." walker overlays enlargements of woodcut plates with her black silkscreen cutout figures in solid black silhouette. in doing so, walker brings to light experiences of racism that were often omitted or merely hinted at in traditional historical narratives. orchid and tropical bonsai show 🌺🪻 through mar 13 | phipps conservatory and botanical gardens the perfect remedy to a pittsburgh winter's chill is a trip to the tropics of phipps — and you'll be immersed in room after room of ephemeral beauty in this year’s orchid and tropical bonsai show. this year's all-new engaging displays create a warm oasis, featuring a vast variety of orchids and classically trained bonsai. you’ll find new ways to appreciate these fascinating specimens as they take centerstage. do you have an upcoming event you'd like featured? add it to our online events calendar !
