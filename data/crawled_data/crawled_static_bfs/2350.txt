officers of the corporation officers of the corporation officers of the corporation consist of the following: chair of the board of trustees, two vice chairs of the board of trustees, the president, chief financial officer, secretary, treasurer and vice presidents. the duties and powers of all officers are determined by the board. the chief financial officer, the treasurer and the secretary will hold office for one year and until their successors shall assume office. the chair and the vice chairs serve for a term of three years. the board determines the length of the term the president serves. the board may dismiss any officer for cause at any time. the election of officers takes place at each annual meeting of the board, but officers may be elected at any meeting to fill vacancies. any two or more offices may be held by the same person. 2024-25 officers of the corporation david a. coulter , chair anne m. molloy , vice chair farnam jahanian , president james h. garrett, jr. ,
