beethoven and brunch sundays: june 23 - july 28, 2024 10:30 a.m. - noon, mellon park, fifth avenue and shady avenue, point breeze/squirrel hill this longtime favorite series entertains music lovers on the lawn at mellon park. satisfy your appetite for classical music by treating yourself to a special sunday morning composed of bach, beethoven and brunch. don't miss the "best brunch" competition during intermission! series presented by citizens and supported by comcast, squirrel hill urban coalition, and wqed-fm 89.3 bach, beethoven and brunch schedule jam at grandview -- new in 2024! saturdays: august 3 - 31, 2024 7 - 8:30 p.m., grandview park bandstand - bailey avenue, mt. washington enjoy live tunes, local brews, and pittsburgh's best views! this brand new concert series features the sparkling downtown skyline as a backdrop on saturday evenings in august. the jam at grandview - jag! - performances combine live rock music with a rotating selection of local ales, ciders and wines to create immaculate vibes on mt. washington. series presented by citizens and supported by the allegheny regional asset district (rad) jam at grandview schedule reservoir of jazz sundays: august 4 - 25, 2024 5 - 7 p.m., highland park, highland avenue and reservoir drive relax on the lawn at highland park to the sounds of jazz performed by pittsburgh's finest musicians. residents of the tri-state area look forward to late summer each year to witness this showcase of the area's extraordinary talent of jazz at the ever-popular reservoir of jazz series. after the concerts, stay for summer soul line dancing . series presented by citizens and supported by aarp pennsylvania, wzum jazz pittsburgh, highland park community council, and the allegheny regional asset district (rad) reservoir of jazz schedule stars at riverview series saturdays: june 8 - july 27, 2024 7 - 8:30 p.m., riverview park, observatory hill "stars at riverview" brings amazing music to riverview park on saturday evenings. so bring a lawn chair or spread a blanket on observatory hill for an entire summer of incredible musical entertainment. after the concerts, stay for dollar bank cinema in the park .
