downtown *construction updates are made monday – friday during business hours. pittsburgh regional transit detours for transit riders, staying up to date with pittsburgh regional transit’s detour information is crucial for getting around seamlessly. prt provides up-to-date detour information for their bus, rail, stations, and park-n-ride facilities . penndot 511pa see what’s happening on penndot’s roadways in real-time by checking out the penndot traffic cams . for specific information about penndot district 11 projects, visit their website . hov lanes the high occupancy vehicle (hovs) lanes on i-279 (parkway north) and i-579 (crosstown boulevard) are open to all outbound traffic on fridays and the weekends after 7 p.m. and remain open until 5 a.m. on mondays. in addition, the two-passenger vehicle rule does not apply during weekend hours. to alleviate congestion and bypass construction, motorists are encouraged to use the hov lanes to exit the city of pittsburgh following games and other events downtown on weekends. weather information + road construction for weather-related and road condition information for southwestern pennsylvania, call 511. share this
