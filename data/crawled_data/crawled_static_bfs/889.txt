of life within urban neighborhoods by supporting cultural expression, social identity, economic development, and, most importantly, a positive and safe sense of place. the pittsburgh downtown partnership has developed an initiative to bring more live music into restaurants, bars, and other unique venues in downtown pittsburgh. click here to view this week’s music lineup recurring music campaign. goals cultivate an expanded, sustainable, and diverse live music scene in downtown pittsburgh to support the downtown business community increase the number and variety of outlets for local musicians in downtown further establish downtown as a default nightlife destination develop consistent audience traffic to warrant continued investment in musical entertainment by participating venues
